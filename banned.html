Compliance Notice

UnlockIt is offline.

As of today, unlockit.ch has been taken offline following enforcement action under European Union regulations. Access to the former site and its services has been permanently disabled. We are cooperating with the relevant authorities and legal counsel.

Reference: EU Compliance Domain: unlockit.ch Status: Permanently Offline

A sincere apology

To developers, artists, publishers, platform teams, moderators, and to our former community: we are deeply sorry. UnlockIt knowingly facilitated access to unauthorized software and content. That decision harmed the people who create the games and tools we claimed to love, and it undermined the trust that keeps a healthy creative ecosystem alive. No explanation can undo that harm, and we accept full responsibility.

We apologize to independent creators whose margins are thin, to studios supporting employees and families, to distributors and storefronts who invest in infrastructure and customer support, and to the broader audience who expect fairness. Our actions created risk and confusion; they incentivized shortcuts over support, and they contributed to a culture that normalizes infringement. We regret this profoundly.

Effective immediately, we have ceased all related operations. We will not relaunch under a new name, mirror, or domain. We are cooperating with counsel to comply with applicable EU law and any lawful requests from rights holders or authorities. Where lawful and appropriate, data necessary for compliance will be retained; otherwise, operational data and prior content have been removed.

We encourage everyone who visited UnlockIt to choose legitimate channels: purchase from official stores, support creators on platforms that compensate them, and consider community programs that offer demos, trials, or open-source alternatives. If you can, fund the work you enjoy. If you cannot, please explore the many legal free options and sales offered by reputable platforms.

Finally, to anyone misled by our messaging in the past: we are sorry. Our previous framing downplayed real consequences. We are committed to learning from this, and to not repeat these mistakes.

— The former UnlockIt team

Why is the site offline?

The domain unlockit.ch has been taken offline following enforcement under EU regulations. Services previously available through this domain have been disabled and will not return.

Will my data be deleted?

We have removed site content and non-essential operational data. Any data required for legal compliance may be retained as advised by counsel. We are not collecting new data and there is no user login.

Will there be a new domain or mirror?

No. We will not create mirrors, forks, or successor projects. Any site or profile claiming to be an UnlockIt relaunch is unaffiliated.

How can rights holders reach you?

If you are a rights holder or authority with a lawful request, please contact our legal point of contact at legal@unlockit.ch.
